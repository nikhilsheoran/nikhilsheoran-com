I'm an 18-year-old student pursuing an Electronics and Communication engineering degree at [BITS Goa](https://www.bits-pilani.ac.in/goa/).

Currently, I'm working on two big life projects: [Media Groww](https://mediagroww.com/) (a YouTube agency) and [FastCut](https://fastcutai.co/) (a software product to create engaging reels quickly).

**This is how it all started:**

Highly inspired by Ali Abdaal and a bunch of other youtubers, I decided to start a [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@codeleyout6175) back in 2021. Initially, wanted to post web dev tutorials, but realized how hard it is, and gave up just after posting the intro video. 🥹

But the desire resurfaced in Feb 2022, when I posted another [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=krWeNv1GvSY) on a new channel. (This time, inspired by [Mike Shake](https://www.youtube.com/@MikeShake) :p)
Soon, Class 12th pre-board exams came by, and I had to, once again give up on my dream of becoming a YouTuber.

Fast Forward to Sept 2022, when I decided it's time to take things seriously. Entrance exams were all done and I was ready to become a YouTuber this time. This marked my entry into video editing, which as I dived deeper into, I started to enjoy a lot.

In December, a senior from my college asked me to edit a [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tlNIHky7ZHI) for his channel. This was a paid gig. I immidiately agreed. And that's how I made my [first dollar](https://twitter.com/_nikhilsheoran/status/1604491342482214913).

I've always been interested in doing something of my own, rather than working for someone else. That [urge](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/sKaP7oxbdAo) led to the beginning of Media Groww in April 2023, along with [Jeet](https://twitter.com/JeetKalita719).

It's been six months since. The agency has grown to 5 active clients, and close to $1k MRR. (that's when 50% ownership hurts 😭🤣) In fact, one of my plans for the next half of the financial year is to at least 5x this number.

Anyway, while editing for clients, I noticed a specific client need: short form video edits like hormozi style [editing](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=X4DNdM8jSQY) among others. This can be automated using code. And that's how the idea for FastCut came around.

I started building FastCut with [Harsh](https://twitter.com/hashtodi). The MVP is almost ready and we'll launch soon. 🥳

Nowadays, I make videos on YouTube, mainly sharing the lessons I learn along the way while trying new stuff. I'm not very consistent, although I try to be.

That was my story so far. If there's something you want to discuss about, the best way to reach me is through [e-mail](mailto:nikhil@nikhilsheoran.com).
